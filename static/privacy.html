Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

Overview

ApplyDraft is a job application automation tool that helps users create and manage job application emails. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

Information We Collect

  • Authentication data: When you sign in with Google or Microsoft, we receive your email address and basic profile information to identify your account.
  • Gmail access: If you connect your Gmail account, we request permission to create email drafts on your behalf. We only use the gmail.compose scope to create drafts in your Gmail account. We do not read, modify, or delete your existing emails.
  • Application data: Job requirements, target firms, generated cover letters, and email templates you create within the app.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your identity and manage your account
  • To create email drafts in your Gmail or Outlook account when you generate job applications
  • To generate personalized cover letters and application materials using AI

What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not read your existing emails
  • We do not send emails on your behalf â€” we only create drafts for your review
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties
  • We do not store your Gmail or Outlook passwords

Data Storage

Your account settings and OAuth tokens are stored securely in Supabase (our database provider). Application data (cover letters, templates) is stored on our server and associated with your user account.

Third-Party Services

  • Google OAuth: For authentication and Gmail draft creation
  • Microsoft OAuth: For authentication and Outlook draft creation
  • Supabase: For database and authentication infrastructure
  • Anthropic (Claude AI): For generating cover letters and searching job postings

Data Deletion

You can disconnect your Gmail or Outlook account at any time from the Settings page, which removes the stored OAuth tokens. To delete your account and all associated data, please contact us.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@applydraft.top.
